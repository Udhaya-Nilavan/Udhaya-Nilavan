What is Rainwater Harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting involves collecting and storing rainwater for later use, helping reduce dependency on traditional water supplies and promoting environmental conservation.

Benefits of Rainwater Harvesting

  • Reduced water bills and costs
  • Promotes sustainable use of natural resources
  • Increases self-sufficiency in water management
